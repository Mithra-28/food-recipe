Chicken Noodles

200g dried egg noodles 250g boneless chicken breast, thinly sliced 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 3 cloves garlic, minced 1-inch piece of ginger, minced 1 cup sliced mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cabbage, etc.) 3 tablespoons soy sauce Salt and pepper to taste Spring onions, chopped for garnish

Shawarma

500g boneless chicken thighs or breast, thinly sliced 1/4 cup plain yogurt 1 teaspoon paprika 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for heat) Salt and black pepper to taste For Serving: Hot sauce (optional, for extra spiciness)

Mutton Briyani

500g mutton (goat meat), cut into pieces 2 cups basmati rice 1 large onion, thinly sliced 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves 1/4 cup fried onions (optional, for garnish) 1/4 cup ghee or vegetable oil 1/4 cup milk Salt to taste 4 cups water

Tava Fish curry

500g fish fillets (any firm-fleshed fish like tilapia, salmon, or cod) 2 large onions, finely chopped 3 tomatoes, chopped, 1-inch piece of ginger, minced 1/2 cup coconut milk 1/2 cup water 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves 1/4 cup vegetable oil 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin A pinch of asafoetida (hing) Salt to taste
