Pasta

250g pasta (e.g., penne, fusilli, or spaghetti) 2 tablespoons olive oil 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 small onion, finely chopped 1 cup chopped mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, broccoli, etc.) 1 teaspoon dried basil 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (optional, for heat) Salt and black pepper to taste Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for garnish) Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)

Masala Dosa

1 cup raw rice 1 cup parboiled rice (idli rice) 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram lentils) 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds Salt to taste A few curry leaves 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder 2 tablespoons vegetable oil Salt to taste

Naan paneer masala

500g mutton (goat meat), cut into pieces 2 cups basmati rice 1 large onion, thinly sliced 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves 1/4 cup fried onions (optional, for garnish) 1/4 cup ghee or vegetable oil 1/4 cup milk Salt to taste 4 cups water

Spring potato

500g baby potatoes 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 1 teaspoon cumin seeds 1 teaspoon red chili powder 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder 1/2 teaspoon garam masala Salt to taste Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
